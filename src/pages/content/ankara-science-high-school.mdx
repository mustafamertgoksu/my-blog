    ## History

    ### Ankara Science High School was established as a result of Turkey's attempt to improve secondary education in the great technology race that countries entered after the Second World War. The Science High School project was carried out by the Ministry of National Education, Ford Foundation, METU and AID (International Development Organization)

    ### The first teachers of the school were selected by the advisors from METU and Ankara University. Selected science teachers were subjected to special training, were sent to the United States, followed the developments in their branches, and prepared lesson programs. The school was opened for education in October 1964 after the construction of the first buildings. The school, which gave its first graduates in 1967, maintained its title as the only science high school in Turkey until 1982.

    ### As a result of their successful work, their graduates have received national and international awards in the fields of science and technology, and have undertaken important duties in the country and abroad.

    ### To direct the knowledge of FL graduates to contribute to the social, scientific and cultural development of the country and FL; Ankara Science High Schools Association was established in 1989, which also includes the purpose of making efforts to spread scientific thinking in the country.

    ## Achievements

     
    ### In recent years, it has been in the top 3 in Turkey in terms of TYT/YKS averages. He also won various degrees in school project competitions. It achieves many successes with the olympic teams selected from among the students through gradual exams. Again, in the high school mathematics competition organized by the Ankara Science High School METU Mathematics Community, he won the first place in all three stages and four of the final competitions, and became the first in Ankara. There are many medals that school students have won in recent years. 

    ### According to the 2019 YKS placement results, 49 of our graduates have been placed in Faculties of Medicine, 33 of them have been placed in Faculties of Engineering, 1 in Dentistry, 1 in Law, 1 in Translation and Interpretation, 2 in Economics, 1 in Pilotage, 1 in Child Development, 1 in International Relations and 1 in Physics. Ankara Science High School, which was established in 1964 with the motto of "a school that will train scholars", managed to make a name for itself once again with the 2018 YKS placement results.[8] Among the graduates of the school, there are faculty members at leading universities in America such as MIT and Caltech.

    ### It is also among the top three schools in the high school entrance exam every year. As a matter of fact, according to the 2020 LGS placement results, it ranked 3rd among the top 100 high schools.
